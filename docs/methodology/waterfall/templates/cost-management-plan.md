# Cost Management Plan (Waterfall)

## Purpose
Define budgeting, baselines, and cost control for phase-based delivery.

## Audience
Project Manager, Finance Partner, PMO.

## Inputs
- Cost baseline (`docs/templates/shared/cost-baseline.xlsx`).
- Shared cost plan (`docs/templates/shared/cost-management-plan.md`).

## 1. Cost Estimation & Budgeting
Describe estimation sources, contingency rules, and phase-level allocations.

## 2. Cost Baseline Approval
Define approval authority and variance thresholds.

## 3. Cost Control & Reporting
Describe earned value management and reporting cadence.

## Completion Checklist
- [ ] Budget and baseline approved.
- [ ] Variance thresholds documented.
- [ ] Reporting cadence agreed.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Cost plan supports gate reporting and financial governance.
- Baseline changes require approved change requests.
