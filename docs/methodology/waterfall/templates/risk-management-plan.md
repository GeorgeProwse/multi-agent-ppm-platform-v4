# Risk Management Plan (Waterfall)

## Purpose
Define risk governance and escalation for sequential delivery phases.

## Audience
Project Manager, Risk Manager, PMO, Sponsors.

## Inputs
- Risk register (`docs/methodology/waterfall/templates/risk-register.xlsx`).
- Risk breakdown structure (`docs/methodology/waterfall/templates/risk-breakdown-structure.yaml`).
- Shared risk plan guidance (`docs/templates/shared/risk-management-plan.md`).

## 1. Phase-Based Risk Reviews
Describe risk workshops at initiation, planning, execution, and closure gates.

## 3. Risk Categories
Use the RBS to classify risks by phase and discipline.

## 4. Probability / Impact Matrix
Define the scoring scale used in the risk register.

## 5. Escalation Thresholds
Identify thresholds that trigger sponsor or steering committee review.

## 6. Monitoring & Reporting
Define cadence for risk register updates and risk reports.

## Completion Checklist
- [ ] Phase-based risk reviews scheduled.
- [ ] Escalation thresholds documented.
- [ ] Register updates aligned to governance cadence.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Risk plan supports waterfall gate approvals.
- Risk reporting is aligned with steering committee needs.
